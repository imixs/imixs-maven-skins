Imixs Workflow provides a framework for the development and management of business applications in a very simple and flexible way. Imixs Workflow allows you to store business data and define the workflow in a business process model, so you can implement your applications faster and more flexible! The Imixs Workflow project focuses on human based workflow. This means that a business process is typically controlled by the user (actor). During a business process Imixs Workflow provides users with all necessary information such as different task lists and process documentation. The Workflow System assists users in starting a new process, finding a document and in the completion of current jobs.
